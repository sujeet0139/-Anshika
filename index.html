Best Guidance

What is usually best for buyers choosing carton boxes?

Best all-round option

5 ply corrugated boxes are usually the safest choice for medium to heavy products, courier movement, and warehouse stacking.

For lightweight products

3 ply boxes reduce packaging cost and work well for garments, cosmetics, and smaller retail items.

For branding

Printed mono cartons or branded outer cartons are better when shelf presentation matters.

For export and heavy load

Heavy-duty corrugated boxes with stronger bursting strength help reduce transit damage.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about carton boxes and orders.

Which carton box is best for courier shipment?

For most courier shipments, 5 ply corrugated boxes are the safer choice because they handle stacking, movement, and medium load better than lighter boxes.

Can I order custom sizes for my product?

Yes. You can share your product dimensions, weight, and quantity, and Anshika Enterprises can recommend a more suitable box size and material strength.

Do you offer plain and printed carton boxes?

Yes. Plain outer boxes, single-color print, and branded printed packaging options can be discussed based on order quantity and use case.

What details should I send for faster quotation?

Send box type, size, quantity, product use, printing requirement, and delivery location. This helps create a faster and more accurate quote.
